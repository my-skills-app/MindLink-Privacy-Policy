Introduction

Thank you for using MindLink. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information to provide our services:

Permissions and Access

Camera Access Our app requires camera permission to capture photos and videos for core app functionality
Microphone Access We use microphone permission to enable voice features such as audio recording, voice commands, and voice messaging
Overlay Permission We use overlay permission to display floating content and notifications over other apps for enhanced user experience
Storage Access We may access device storage to save and retrieve user-generated content

Device and Usage Information

  • Device Information: Basic device information such as device type, operating system version, and hardware specifications
  • App Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app, including features used and time spent
  • Performance Data: Crash reports and performance metrics to help us improve the app

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To provide and maintain core app functionality
  • Feature Enhancement: To enable camera, voice, and overlay features
  • User Experience: To personalize and improve your app experience
  • Technical Support: To diagnose and fix technical issues
  • Performance Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and optimize app performance
  • Security: To protect against fraud and ensure app security

3. Data Storage and Security

Data Storage

⚠️ Important: We do not store or share your personal media files (photos, audio, video recordings) on external servers unless explicitly required for app functionality.
  • User-generated content is stored locally on your device by default
  • Any data that is stored on our servers is encrypted and protected

Data Security

  • We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data
  • Data transmission uses secure encryption protocols
  • Access to user data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • We regularly review and update our security practices
